Submission Guidelines

Paper submission & review policy
Authors are advised to submit their original and unpublished work (related to the conference theme).
LOREM EPSUM
Also, note that the (HYDCON) team takes care of plagiarism (even self-plagiarism) as serious professional misconduct. All papers will be screened for plagiarism, and the (HYDCON) team will reject them in case there exists significant overlap with the previously published papers/papers.
Template (for papers)
All papers submitted to the 2024 IEEE HYDCON must be written in English and formatted in the standard IEEE Xplore double-column format (A4 size), you can download templates here.https://www.ieee.org/conferences/publishing/templates.html
Submission guidelines
Three different types of submissions are expected: 1) regular paper, 2) invited paper, and 3) position paper.

Important Note

Regardless of submission types, authors are always advised to submit their original and unpublished work (related to the conference theme).
